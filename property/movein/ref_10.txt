A three double bedroom period house renovated and extended to an exemplary standard.
This Victorian house has been transformed by the current owner, creating a perfect blend of modern living and period charm. Finished to a very high specification, using the very best materials and flair for interior design. 
On the ground floor there's a beautifully appointed through-lounge with two elegant fireplaces and several built-in bespoke storage cupboards. The rear of the house has recently been opened up and now boasts a wonderful new kitchen, dining area, bi-folding doors opening onto a secluded rear garden. Upstairs consists of three good size double bedrooms, a recently fitted family bathroom, an additional shower room and a study.
