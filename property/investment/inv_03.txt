End of terrace house occupying a corner position on Herne Hill at the Junction of Ruskin Walk. The property has been extended by the present owners but could benefit from further improvement.
The property is spacious with a gross internal area of 2,385 sq ft (221.6 sq m). At the rear there is a south east facing garden with the undoubted benefit of a large garage with access from Ruskin Walk.
Currently arranged with five bedrooms, two bathrooms (one en suite with restricted headroom) Double reception room, kitchen and dining room. The house occupies and elevated position with good southerly views to the rear.
