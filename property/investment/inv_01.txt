This large Edwardian home comes to the market offering plenty of scope for improvement whilst still retaining many original features. Kept in wonderful condition with four large bedrooms, three reception rooms and two bathrooms, the property also benefits from off street parking and a good sized rear garden.
Within a good school catchment area, the property is located close to the excellent transport links of both North Dulwich BR and Herne Hill BR/Thameslinklink as well as being within easy proximity of Denmark Hill Overground/BR station.
Dulwich Village and the many restaurants, cafes and shops the village offers are within close proximity as well as Herne Hill with its variety of excellent restaurants, bars and cafes and the well known Brockwell Lido and park. The Sunday market offers a varied array of food and gift stalls.
