This four bedroom end terraced house is nestled in a quiet residential cul-de-sac and offers spacious living accommodation throughout. The property benefits from having a porch, kitchen/diner awash with natural light, homely lounge, four good size bedrooms and a bathroom. Willingale Close benefits from close proximity to Woodford Central Line Station, shopping facilities and is a mere short walk to Ray Lodge Primary school, which is currently ranked as 'Outstanding' by Ofsted. The property is full of potential and has ample room to extend (subject to planning consent). To the exterior there is off street parking for a number of cars and a beautiful over 90ft rear garden which has a patio area for entertaining leading to lawn and mature shrubs with summer house. This immaculate house is ready to make for a family home and a viewing comes highly recommended.
